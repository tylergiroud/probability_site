Building Lectern

March 2026

Lectern started with a simple observation: AI models are becoming the front door to the internet, and most brands have no idea how to show up there.

We'd spent years building Presscart, connecting brands to publishers for offsite content. We understood the mechanics of getting brands mentioned on high-trust sites. When ChatGPT and Gemini started citing those same publications in their answers, the connection was obvious.

The problem

Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's algorithm. But AI models don't work the same way. They synthesize answers from hundreds of sources, weighting authority, trust signals, and content quality in ways that are fundamentally different from PageRank.

Brands were asking us: how do we get recommended by ChatGPT? How do we show up when someone asks Gemini for the best product in our category? There was no good answer.

What Lectern does

Lectern is the offsite content engine for AI visibility. We help brands get published on the sites that AI models trust and cite. Not through ads or sponsored content labels, but through genuine, high-quality editorial placement.

The result: when someone asks an AI model about your category, your brand shows up in the answer. Not because you gamed a system, but because you built real visibility across the sources these models rely on.

Where we're headed

We're still early. The AI search landscape is evolving fast, and we're evolving with it. But the core thesis hasn't changed: brands that build real visibility across trusted sources will win in an AI-first world.

The brands that show up in AI answers today will be the default choices tomorrow.

More to come on this. We're just getting started.
